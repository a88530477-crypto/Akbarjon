## 🚨 Problem Statement (Why is this system needed?)

*   **📞📄 Manual Order Processing:** Currently relies on phone calls and paper tickets.
    *   ➡ Leads to a **30% order error rate** ❌
    *   ➡ Results in customer complaints during peak hours 😠
*   **📦 No Real-Time Inventory Visibility:**
    *   ➡ Frequent stock-outs of popular items ⚠
    *   ➡ Leads to inefficient purchasing decisions 💸
*   **👤 No Customer Loyalty System:**
    *   ➡ No order history or personalized marketing 🎯
    *   ➡ Difficult customer retention 📉

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

1.  **🌐 Design a secure, user-friendly online ordering platform** for customers to browse the menu, customize orders, and make digital payments.
2.  **🖥 Develop an internal management dashboard** for staff to track inventory and generate daily sales reports, streamlining order processing.
3.  **🗄 Create a customer database** to store order history, manage loyalty points, and enable targeted promotions.

---

## 📌 Scope of the System

### ✅ In-Scope

*   **👤 Customer Portal:**
    *   🔐 Registration / Login
    *   📋 Interactive Menu
    *   🛒 Customizable Cart
    *   💳 Online Payment (Card)
    *   🚚 Order Tracking (Preparing → Baking → Out for Delivery → Delivered)
*   **👨‍🍳 Kitchen / Staff Dashboard:**
    *   📥 Digital Order Queue
    *   🔄 Order Status Updates
    *   ⚠ Low-Stock Alerts
    *   📊 Simple Order Analytics
*   **👨‍💼 Manager Admin Panel:**
    *   ✏ Full Menu Management
    *   📦 Inventory Tracking
    *   📈 Detailed Sales Reports
    *   👥 Customer Database View

### ❌ Out-of-Scope

*   📱 Native mobile applications (iOS/Android)
*   🚗 Integration with third-party delivery services (Yandex Go, etc.)
*   💼 Employee payroll & scheduling
*   📹 Real-time kitchen video streaming
